## Design Context

### Users
Teens and couples using the app primarily in the evening to "chill". The app serves as a private, shared space for emotional connection and collaborative wishing.

### Brand Personality
**Cute, Private, Emotional.**
The personality is intimate, warm, and sentimental. It avoids being purely transactional or utility-focused, aiming instead to feel like a digital sanctuary for relationships.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Chill Night Vibes.**
- **Theme**: Deep Dark Mode optimized for evening viewing.
- **Visuals**: Soft glows, deep magenta/pink accents, and semi-transparent "glass" surfaces.
- **Anti-references**: No rigid admin dashboards, dense tables, or industrial/corporate UI. Layouts should be spacious and immersive.

### Design Principles
1. **Low-Light Comfort**: Use deep, perceptually uniform dark backgrounds (OKLCH) with soft, glowing accents that won't cause eye strain at 10 PM.
2. **Emotional Presence**: Prioritize rounded geometry and organic shapes over sharp corners. Use meaningful micro-animations (like "heart beats" or "gentle fades") to reinforce the emotional tone.
3. **Immersive Storytelling**: Layouts should feel like a shared journal or scrapbook. Avoid sidebars and technical navigation where possible; keep the focus on the content and the connection.
4. **Playful Sophistication**: Balance the "cute" vibe with high-quality typography and refined spacing so it feels premium and intentional, not like a toy.
